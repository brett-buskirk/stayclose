StayClose Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: StayClose is designed with privacy at its core. All your personal information stays on your device - we never collect, store, or share your data with anyone.

Introduction

StayClose ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the StayClose mobile application.

Information We Collect

Contacts Information

  • We access your device contacts only when you explicitly choose to import them using our contact import feature
  • We store selected contact information (names, phone numbers, email addresses) locally on your device
  • No contact information is transmitted to external servers or third parties

Camera and Photo Access

  • We request camera permission to allow you to take profile photos for your contacts
  • We access your photo gallery to let you select existing images for contact profiles
  • Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared

Important Dates

  • You can manually add important dates (birthdays, anniversaries) for your contacts
  • This information is stored locally on your device only

How We Use Your Information

  • Contact Management: To help you organize and manage your personal relationships
  • Daily Reminders: To provide daily contact suggestions based on your stored contacts
  • Important Date Notifications: To remind you of upcoming birthdays and anniversaries
  • App Functionality: To enable core features like calling, texting, and emailing your contacts

Data Storage and Security

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers
  • No data is shared with third parties or external services
  • Your information remains under your complete control

Permissions Explained

  • Contacts: To import contacts from your device when you choose to do so
  • Camera: To take profile photos for your contacts
  • Storage/Photos: To access and save profile photos
  • Notifications: To send you daily reminders and important date alerts

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually remove contacts
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data
  • You can manually delete individual contacts and their associated data at any time

Third-Party Services

StayClose does not integrate with or share data with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

StayClose is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" above.
